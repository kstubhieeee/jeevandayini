About Jeevandayini

We are dedicated to revolutionizing blood donation and availability through technology and community engagement.

Our Mission

To create a seamless connection between blood donors and recipients, ensuring that no life is lost due to blood shortage.

Our Vision

To build a world where every person has access to safe and sufficient blood supply when needed.

Our Values

Commitment to saving lives, transparency in operations, and dedication to community welfare.
